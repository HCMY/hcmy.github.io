上海合作组织(SCO)青岛峰会祝词

发表于 2018-06-18

生词还挺多啊…不过，我感受到了祖国的自信啊！从英军轰开我国门开始，到现在的军舰访英，好心酸。

Distinguished colleagues, Dear guests, Ladies and gentlemen, Friends, Good evening!

尊敬的各位同事，各位来宾，女士们，先生们，朋友们：大家晚上好！

It is such a pleasure to have you with us here in Qingdao, Shandong Province, on the shore of the Yellow Sea. I wish to extend, on behalf of the Chinese government and people and in my own name, a very warm welcome to all of you, particulary the state leaders and heads of international organizations who have come to attend the meeting of Council of Heads of Member States of the SCO.

很高兴同各位同各位相聚在黄海之滨的青岛。首先，我仅代表中国政府和中国人民，并以我个人的名义，对来华出席上海合作组织成员国元首理事会会议的各国领导人和国际组织负责人,对各位来宾,表示热烈的欢迎!

Shangdong is the home province of Confucius and birthplace of Confucianism. An integral part of Chinese civilization, Confucianism believes that “a just cause shoule be pursued for common good” and ”champions harmony, unity and a shared community for all nations“.

上东是孔子的故乡和儒家文化的发祥地。儒家思想是中华文明的重要组成部分。儒家倡导 ”大道之行，天下为公“， 主张 ”协和万邦，和衷共济，四海一家“。

Its emphasis on unity and harmony has much in common with Shanghai Spirit, namely, mutual trust, mutual benefit, equality, consultation, respect for diverse civilizations and pursuit of common development. The Shanhai Spirit’s focus on seeking common ground while setting aside differences and pursuing mutually beneficial cooperation has own widespread international endorsement and support.

这种“和合”理念同“上海精神”有很多相通之处。“上海精神”坚持互信、互利、平等、协商、尊重多样文明、谋求共同发展,强调求同存异、合作共赢,在国际上获得广泛认同和支持。

Guide by the Shanhai Spirit, The SCO has delivered fruitful outcomes in security, economic and cultural cooperation and made historic strides in institution building. With eight member states, four observer states and six dialohue partners, The SCO has become an important force for upholding regional security, promoting common development and imporving global goverance.

在“上海精神”引领下,本组织丌仅在安全、经济、人文等合作领域取得 丰硕成果,在机制建设方面也迈出历史性步伐。如今,上海合作组织拥有 8 个成员国、4 个观察员国、6 个对话伙伴,是维护地区安全、促进共同发展、完善全球治理的重要力量 。

Qingdao is a famous international sailing captial. It is from here that many ships set sail in pursuit of dreams. Tomorrow, we will hold the first summit of the SCO after its expansion and draw up a blueprint for its future growth.

青岛是世界著名的“帆船之都”,许多船只从这里扬帆起航、追逐梦想。明天,我们将在这里丼行上海合作组织扩员后的首次峰会,全面规划本组织未来发展蓝图。

The Qingdap summit is a new departure point of us. Together, let us hosit the sail of the Shanhai Spirit, break waves and embark on a new voyage for our oganization.

让我们以青岛峰会为新的起点,高扬“上海精神”的风帆,齐心协力,乘风破浪,共同开启上海合作组织发展新征程!

Now, please join us in a toast. To the full success of the Qingdao summit, To the prosperity of our countries and happiness of our people, To a brighter future of SCO, And to the health of all of you and your families, Cheers.

现在,我提议,大家共同丼杯,为青岛峰会囿满成功, 为各国发展繁荣、人民幸福安康, 为上海合作组织的美好明天,为各位来宾和家人的健康,干杯!
