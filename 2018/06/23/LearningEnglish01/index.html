What is depression

发表于 2018-06-23

Depression is the leading cause of disability in the world. In the United States, close to 10 percent of adults struggle with depression. But because it’s
a mental illness, it can be a lot harder to understand than, say, high cholesterol.

抑郁症是世界上造成人类失能的首要原因。在美国，接近%10的成年人被抑郁症所困扰。但是因为抑郁症是一种心理疾病，要了解他比，比如说胆固醇难很多。

One major source of confusion is the difference between having depression and just feeling depressed. Almost everyone feels down from time to time. Getting a bad grade, losing a job, having an argument, even a rainy day can bring on feelings of sadness.

其中最常造成混淆的地方就是患抑郁症和单纯的和情绪低落不同，几乎每个人都会有失落的时候，成绩不好，失业， 争吵，甚至下雨天也会引起悲伤的感觉。

Sometimes there’s no trigger at all, it just pops up out of the blue, The circumstances change, and those feelings disappear. Clinical depression is different. It’a a medical disorder, and it won’t go away just because you want it to.

有时候完全没有诱发原因，难过的感觉就这样冒出来，然后随着情况改变，这些感觉也就消失了。临床抑郁症则不一样，那是一种疾病，他不会因为你要他消失就消失

It lingers for at least two consecutive weeks and significantly interferes with one’s ability to work, play or love. Depression can have a lot of different symptoms: a low mood, loss of interest in things you’d normaly enjoy, changes in appetite, feeling worthless or excessively guilty, sleeping either too much or too littile, poor concertration, restlessness or slowness, lose of energy, or recurrent thughts of suicide.

低落的情绪至少会持续两周，而且严重干扰一个人的工作，玩或者爱人的能力。抑郁症可以有不同的症状：情绪低落，对平常喜欢的事物失去兴趣，胃口改变，嗜睡或者熬夜，注意力难以集中，焦虑或迟滞，失去活力或者有自杀的念头。

If you have at least five of those symptoms, according to psychiatric guidelines, you qualify for a diagnosis of depression. And it’s not just behavioral symptoms. Depression has physicial manifestations inside the brain. First of all, there are changes that could be seen with the naked eye and X-ray vision. These include smaller frontal lobels and hippocampal volumns.

如果你拥有至少5个症状，从精神科上来说，你已经符合抑郁症的标准。而不仅仅是行为上的症状，抑郁症在大脑中也有具体表现。首先，这些变化能通过肉眼和X射线观察到，包括很小的额叶和海绵体。

On a more micro scale, depression is associated with a few things: the abnormal transmissions or depletion of certain neuro transmitters, especially serotonin(血清素), Noradrenaline(去甲肾上腺素) and dopamine(多巴胺). Blunted circadian(昼夜节奏的) rhythms, or specific changes in th REM and slow-wave parts of your sleep.

更微观来看，抑郁症和几件事有关： 神经传导物质的不正常传递或减少，特别是血清素，去甲肾上腺素和多巴胺。直接打乱你的生理时钟，或快速眼动期和慢波期明显改变。
